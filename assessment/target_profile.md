\# Target Profile (6-12 Month Horizon)

|---|---:|---:|---|

\## What “good” looks like for Northbridge

Within 6-12 months, Northbridge should operate a repeatable and evidence-driven cybersecurity program that:

\- demonstrates reliable control operation for customer assurance,

\- quickly detects and contains high-probability threats,

\- recovers critical services against tested objectives,

\- tracks security improvements through governance metrics.



\## Target state characteristics

\- Quarterly cyber risk reviews tied to business and supplier change events.

\- Full inventory ownership and classification for critical assets/datasets.

\- Measurable patching, MFA, and EDR coverage with exception governance.

\- Prioritized detection use cases mapped to top threat scenarios.

\- Incident playbooks exercised with legal/communications stakeholders.

\- Recovery drills demonstrating restoration of portal and core warehouse processes.
